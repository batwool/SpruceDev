About Spruce Web Development

Our Story

We started this company because we kept seeing the same problem: too many local businesses and tradesmen were paying good money for websites that were just… average. These sites were often clunky, outdated, and didn’t show off the real quality of the work behind the business.

That didn’t sit right with us. A great business deserves a great website. Your website should feel professional, make it easy for customers to reach you, and actually help your business grow — not hold it back.

That’s why we’re here. We create high-quality websites that truly represent your business, giving you an online presence that’s clear, modern, and built to last. No more settling for “good enough.”
