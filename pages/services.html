Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our services

How long does it take to build a website?

Typically, our websites are completed within 2-4 weeks depending on the complexity and how quickly we receive your content.

Do I need to host my website somewhere?

We can handle hosting for you or recommend reliable hosting providers. Our preferred hosting partners offer optimized performance for small businesses.

Will my website work on mobile devices?

Yes! All our websites are built with a mobile-first approach and are fully responsive on all devices.

Can I update the website myself?

Absolutely. We provide training and can build your site on a content management system that allows easy updates.

Do you offer ongoing support?

Yes, we offer various support packages to keep your website running smoothly and securely.

What information do you need from me to get started?

We'll need your business information, photos, service descriptions, and any special requirements you have for your website.
